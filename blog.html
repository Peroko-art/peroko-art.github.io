What's been on my mind?

Living your life facilitates art.

When I look at art that inspires me, I tend to think "Oh, why didn't I think of that?" or "How did the author conceptualize it? How long did it take them? What are all the things they had to take into account when making it?" It seems typical for one to look at the piece as a product to be consumed, rather than a remaining record of a creative process. However, I believe art's purpose really is self-expression. I recently spoke with an artist of many decades and we had a discussion on what art is. What I walked away with was a sense that not even someone with a degree and years of experience can accurately define art. To me, body piercing and game development are arts; to a classical pianist, they may not be.

Stay delusional.
